# Task 1

**Brief Description**

For this task, I created a digital literacy presentation to explain the concept in a simple and structured way so that students can easily understand its importance in everyday life.

In my slides, I started by defining digital literacy as the ability to find, evaluate, and communicate information using digital platforms. Then I included sections like “What is Digital Literacy,” key dimensions such as data privacy and algorithmic awareness, and examples of useful digital tools like Google Docs, Notion, Excel, GitHub, and Canva. I also added a section on safe internet practices, where I covered topics like verification of information, security through strong passwords and 2FA, privacy management, and maintaining ethical behavior online.

While designing the slides, I tried to keep the layout clean and visually balanced with consistent colours and minimal text so that the content remains easy to read. One small challenge I faced was organizing different topics without making the slides feel overloaded, especially while covering multiple aspects like tools and safety practices.

Overall, this task helped me better understand digital literacy concepts and also improved my ability to present information in a clear and visually effective way.
