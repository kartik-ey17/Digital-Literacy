Part B: Social Media Etiquette Checklist

• Do’s

Always think carefully before posting, as it reflects your online identity.
Maintain a professional image, especially on platforms related to academics or career growth.
Adjust your privacy settings to manage who can view your content and personal details.
Post content that is helpful, positive, and adds value.
Be respectful while interacting with others in comments, messages, or discussions.

• Don’ts

Avoid sharing sensitive personal details like your phone number, address, or passwords.
Do not post content that is offensive, disrespectful, or inappropriate.
Never share false or unverified information.
Avoid using weak or repeated passwords across different accounts.
Do not participate in online conflicts, arguments, or cyberbullying.
