Part B: Stay Safe Online (Prevention Checklist)

General Safety Tips

• Always verify the sender before approving any UPI payment request. If anything seems unusual, do not continue.

• Keep in mind that entering your UPI PIN means money is being sent, not received.

• Do not click on unfamiliar links or download files from unknown or untrusted sources.

• Regularly update your banking apps and mobile software to stay protected from security threats.

• Use strong, unique passwords for different apps and never share them with others.

• Avoid making financial transactions on public Wi-Fi, as it may not be secure.

UPI & Financial Safety Tips

• Never disclose your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details to anyone, even if they claim to be from an official source.

• Do not approve payment requests from unknown individuals, especially during online selling or buying.

• Always check the payment status directly in your app instead of relying on screenshots.

• Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or suspicious sources, as they may lead to unauthorized transactions.

Reporting & Awareness

• If you experience any fraud, report it immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in).

• You can also contact the Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 for quick support.
